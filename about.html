Building the Future of Tech, One Member at a Time

Premier University Computer Club (PUCC) is a vibrant community of tech enthusiasts, innovators, and problem-solvers dedicated to advancing computer science education and practice since 2015.

Our Mission

To empower students with practical computer science skills, foster a culture of innovation and competition, and create pathways to successful careers in technology through hands-on learning, mentorship, and community collaboration.

visibility

Our Vision

To become the premier hub for technological excellence in Bangladesh, producing world-class developers, researchers, and innovators who lead the digital transformation of our nation and beyond.

Our Journey

A Decade of Growth and Impact

2015

Founded with a Vision

A small group of passionate students came together to form the Premier University Computer Club with just 15 members.

2017

First National Competition

PUCC hosted its first inter-university programming contest, attracting teams from across Bangladesh.

2020

University Recognition

Officially recognized by Premier University as an approved student organization, expanding our reach and resources.

2023

National Champions

PUCC members won 8+ gold medals across various national competitions, establishing our reputation.
